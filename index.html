# Privacy Policy for Account Change Manager **Last Updated: November 9, 2025** ## Overview Account Change Manager is an internal tool designed for employees to safely manage client accounts. This privacy policy explains what data we collect and how it's used. ## Data Collection ### 1. Chrome Profile Email Address - **What we collect:** Your Chrome profile email address - **Why we collect it:** To verify you are an authorized employee and to determine your admin permissions - **Where it's stored:** Temporarily in Chrome's local storage and recorded in our company Google Sheets when you take actions - **How long we keep it:** Cached for 24 hours for admin status checks ### 2. Button Interaction Data - **What we collect:** When admins disable buttons, we collect: - Page URL where the button is located - CSS selector of the button - Button text/label - Your email address (who disabled it) - Timestamp - **Why we collect it:** To maintain a centralized list of disabled buttons across all team members - **Where it's stored:** Company-owned Google Sheets - **Who can access it:** Only employees with access to the Google Sheet ### 3. Usage Data - **What we collect:** Extension usage is logged in Google Apps Script execution logs - **Why we collect it:** For debugging and monitoring extension functionality - **Where it's stored:** Google Apps Script execution logs (auto-deleted after a period) - **Who can access it:** Only the Google Sheet owner and authorized admins ## Data We Do NOT Collect - We do not collect browsing history - We do not collect passwords or credentials - We do not collect personal information beyond email - We do not track your behavior outside of button blocking actions - We do not share any data with third parties - We do not sell any data - We do not use data for advertising ## Data Storage All data is stored in: - **Company-owned Google Sheets:** Accessible only by employees with proper permissions - **Chrome local storage:** Temporary caching of button lists and user authentication status (cleared on extension removal) ## Data Security - Extension only works for company email addresses - All communication with Google Sheets API is encrypted (HTTPS) - Admin permissions managed through company-controlled spreadsheet - No data transmitted to external services ## User Rights - You can view all collected data by accessing the Google Sheet (link in admin panel) - You can request removal of your email from blocked button records by contacting an admin - You can uninstall the extension at any time, which clears local cache ## Data Retention - **Button blocking records:** Retained indefinitely for compliance purposes - **Admin status cache:** 24 hours - **Button list cache:** 5 minutes (or until manually refreshed) ## Third-Party Services This extension uses: - **Google Apps Script:** To host the API backend - **Google Sheets:** To store configuration and button blocking data Both services are operated by Google and subject to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy). ## Changes to This Policy We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document. ## Compliance This extension complies with: - Chrome Web Store Developer Program Policies - companies internal data handling policies - GDPR and privacy best practices --- **Note:** This extension is an internal tool for employees only. It is not intended for public use and will only function with company email addresses.
